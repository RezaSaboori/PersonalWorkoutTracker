## **Phase 1: Foundation Building**

This phase is all about mastering proper form, getting your body used to new movements, and establishing a consistent routine. The nutrition and hydration schedule is the same for both workout and rest days; only the main activity changes.

### **Week 1: Daily Schedule**

**Applies to:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday

#### **Workout Day Plan**

| Time | Activity | Detailed Plan & Expert Recommendations |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **7:00 AM** | **Wake Up & Hydrate** | Drink a large glass of water (500ml / 16oz) immediately upon waking. |
| **7:30 AM** | **Breakfast** | **Focus:** Lean protein, complex carbs, healthy fats. \<br\> **Example:** 3 scrambled eggs, 1/2 avocado, and a bowl of oatmeal (approx. 40g dry) with berries. |
| **12:30 PM** | **Lunch** | **Focus:** Balanced meal. \<br\> **Example:** Grilled chicken breast (150g / 5oz), a large mixed green salad, and a serving of quinoa (approx. 1 cup cooked). |
| **4:00 PM** | **Pre-Workout Meal** | **Focus:** Complex carbs & protein. \<br\> **Example:** Greek yogurt with almonds. **Hydration:** Drink 500ml (16oz) of water. |
| **5:30 PM** | **Pre-Workout Snack** | **Focus:** Simple carbs for energy. \<br\> **Example:** A banana. **Hydration:** Drink 250ml (8oz) of water. |
| **6:00 PM \- 6:30 PM** | **Workout Session** | **Total Duration:** 25-30 minutes. \<br\> **1\. Warm-up (5 mins):** Dynamic stretching (arm circles, leg swings). \<br\> **2\. Main Circuit (15 mins):** Complete 2 sets, resting 60-90s between exercises. \<br\>  	\- Bodyweight Squats: 12-15 reps \<br\>  	\- Push-ups (on knees or toes): 8-12 reps \<br\>  	\- Lunges (alternating): 10-12 reps per leg \<br\>  	\- Plank: Hold for 20-30 seconds \<br\>  	\- Jumping Jacks: 30 seconds \<br\>  	\- Glute Bridges: 15-20 reps \<br\> **3\. Cool-down (5 mins):** Static stretching (hold each for 20-30s). |
| **Immediately Post-Workout** | **Recovery Shake** | **Focus:** Protein & simple carbs. \<br\> **Example:** 1 scoop of whey protein with a pear. **Hydration:** Drink at least 500ml (16oz) of water. |
| **8:00 PM** | **Dinner** | **Focus:** Protein for muscle repair. \<br\> **Example:** Baked salmon (150g / 5oz) with steamed vegetables. |
| **10:30 PM** | **Sleep** | Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep. |
