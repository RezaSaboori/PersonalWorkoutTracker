## ***Phase 2: Strength Building***

*In this phase, the frequency increases to 4 workout days per week and you'll introduce more challenging exercises to focus on building strength.*

| ***6:00 PM \- 6:35 PM*** | ***Workout Session*** | ***Total Duration:** 30-35 minutes. \<br\> **1\. Warm-up (5 mins):** Dynamic stretching. \<br\> **2\.
